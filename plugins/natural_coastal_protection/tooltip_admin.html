Flood Extent

Area potentially inundated with 3m sea level rise. Determined from 2007 projections plus 3 meters of sea level rise.

Population

Count and percentage of population within the flood extent estimated using WorldPop 2016 data of people per 100 meter pixel and people per hectare. Source

Illiteracy

Percent of total population with no schooling. From Statistical Institute of Jamaica (STATIN)

Roads

Length of roads within the flood extent. Roads digitized and verified in 2019, from Open Street Map (OSM)

Critical Facilities

Number of critical facilities in flood extent (water/waste treatment, cell towers, electric, airport etc). Data were downloaded and compiled from OpenStreet Map (OSM)
in March 2019 by BYU students for TNC.

Emergency Facilities

Number of emergency infrastructure in flood extent (medical, shelter, fire, police etc.) 2009 -2010 data compiled from various sources including Planning Institute of Jamaica, Ministry of Health and Mona GeoInformatics Institute, and Jamaica Fire Brigade.

Communicty Facilities

Number of community facilities in flood extent (religious, hotel, tourism, heritage). From 2006 protected area gap assessment for the country of Jamaica led by The Nature Conservancy.

Reef Area

Total area (sq km) of coral reef within a 5km buffer and 1km of coastal area. As part of the Caribbean Large Marine Ecosystem project in 2013, data digitized, validated against high resolution imagery, and combined with other data including "Tropical Coral Reefs of the World (2011)" from the World's Resources Institute.

Mangrove Area

Total area (sq km) of mangrove within a 5km buffer and 1km of coastal area. As part of the Caribbean Large Marine Ecosystem project in 2013, data digitized, validated against high resolution imagery, and combined with other data including "World Atlas of Mangroves (2012)" from Mark Spalding.

Seagrass Area

Total area (sq km) of seagrass within a 5km buffer and 1km of coastal area. As part of the Caribbean Large Marine Ecosystem project in 2013, data digitized, validated against high resolution imagery, and combined with other data including the "World Atlas of Seagrasses (2003)" from the UN Environment Programme â€“ World Conservation Monitoring Centre.

Mangrove Change

Positive or negative change in mangrove coverage between 2013 - 2018. From normalized difference vegetation index (NDVI) analysis on Landsat 8 satellite data between 2013-2018.
